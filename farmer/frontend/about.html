Connecting Roots, Growing Futures.

We are a team passionate about empowering local farmers and building a more sustainable, transparent food ecosystem for everyone.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between the hardworking farmers of our community, like those right here in Bhind, and the consumers who seek fresh, high-quality produce. We believe in leveraging technology to create a fair marketplace that benefits both the producer and the buyer.

KrishiMitra was born from a desire to see farmers get the true value for their labor while providing businesses and families with direct access to the freshest food imaginable.
